This was an excellent chat with my Canadian friend, [Michael Greenspan](https://www.linkedin.com/in/michaelgreenspan/), about my experience with ADHD. We explored how my diagnosis reshaped my career, the strengths and struggles of working in tech with ADHD, and how we can better support tech professionals with ADHD.

**What we covered:**

- How my ADHD diagnosis helped me understand my career path
- The challenges and advantages of ADHD in software engineering
- The tools, strategies, and accommodations that help ADHD professionals thrive
